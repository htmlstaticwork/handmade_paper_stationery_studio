The Edge. The Fiber.

A Decidedly Tactile Experience

There is No substitute for the hand. Our Sage collection features a true natural deckle—the feathered, untamed border formed only at the moment of the vat pull. When your fingers meet these fibers, you'll understand why we refuse to trim our most precious lots.

For the Scribe

Internally Sized for Ink

Unlike raw pulp, our paper is delicately sized to ensure compatibility with traditional fountain pen inks, calligraphy nibs, and heavy letterpress impressions. Every stroke remains crisp, resisting spidering and bleed-through while celebrating the slight resistance of the cotton fluff.

Earth's Own Palette

Archival Cotton Heritage

Pulped from 100% recycled cotton textile offcuts, our 300 GSM Sage suite is naturally archival. It contains no bleach, no acid, and no artificial lignin. It is paper built to be archived—holding your memories as vividly in a century as it does today.
