Our Studio Story

"We believe paper should breathe, hold memory, and whisper to the touch."

At Foliage Studio, every sheet is pulled by hand, slowly dried, and meticulously inspected. We merge traditional vat-making techniques with contemporary design to create heirloom quality pieces.

There is magic in the imperfections—the deckled edges, the stray botanical fragments, the subtle variations in thickness. This is where character is born.

The Artisan Difference

Why Foliage Studio?

100% Cotton Rag

Archival quality, acid-free paper pulped from textile offcuts. Kind to the earth, magnificent to hold.

Botanical Inclusions

Real, hand-foraged leaves and petals trapped within the fibers. Each sheet is a unique landscape.

Deckled Edges

True deckle edges naturally formed at the vat, offering that authentic, untamed organic finish.

Calligraphy Ready

Internally sized for nib and ink compatibility, preventing spidering and bleeding on your custom suites.
